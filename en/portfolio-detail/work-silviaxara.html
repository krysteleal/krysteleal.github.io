About the project

The work I did on Sílvia Xará's website was gratifying. I worked with Sílvia in the creation of her new website, as well as in the production of her brand image. It was total teamwork. I received her ideas and materialized them into a complete branding, which is deconstructed in the different icons and representations of Sílvia's work.

The website was developed not only to present the services but also prepared for the selling and distribution of digital products. I worked not only in the construction of the website (WordPress) but also in the implementation of the selling integration, using Woocmmerce, where Silvia can sell her meditations. I also created her branding (logo, iconography, and branding).
