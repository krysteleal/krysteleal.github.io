About the project

When I arrived in California in early 2017, the Acontece no Vale project was one of my sources of information. I got in touch with Waldana to ask for information about health insurance. And, because I have a small professional bio on my email signature, Waldana asked me for a quote.

I ended up developing the Acontece no Vale website! It is a pleasure to be behind the site that is a useful guide about California written in Portuguese.
