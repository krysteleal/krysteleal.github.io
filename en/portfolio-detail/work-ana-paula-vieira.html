About the Project

Ana's work involves mourning, overcoming, and looking for light. Her website needed to reflect that. With face-to-face and online services, we had to create an easy way so that the contact-making was intuitive for anyone who enters the site.

With light colors that reflect the light and brightness, Ana's website is informative but also inspiring with content published on her blog space.
