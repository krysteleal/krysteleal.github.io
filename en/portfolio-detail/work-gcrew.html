GCREW

About the Project

When Cláudia Soeiro contacted me, we had an immediate connection. Two Portuguese women who live abroad, but with a desire to continue adding value to our country. The GCREW is a project for women who feel more than ever the need to sit down, with a hot drink or a glass of wine, and share, speak, learn from other women, and feel the mind expand with sharing—an old fashioned social club, but adapted to the modern woman.

The work developed with GCREW included creating the website, optimizing the content, and organizing all ideas in a way that made sense to the target client of the project.


Client: Cláudia Soeiro - GCREW
